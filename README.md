### 🛠 Research Interests:
- 🧪 Laser powder bed fusion of stainless steels and engineering alloys  
- ⚙️ Super duplex stainless steels, including UNS S32750 / SDSS 2507  
- 🔬 Microstructure–property–corrosion relationships  
- 🧫 Electrochemical corrosion analysis: OCP, potentiodynamic polarization, and EIS  
- 🧭 Phase balance, crystallographic texture, and residual stress  
- 🔥 Heat treatment, post-processing effects, and secondary-phase formation  
- 💻 Scientific computing, data analysis, and materials informatics using Python and MATLAB
